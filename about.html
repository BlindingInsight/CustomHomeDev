Our Philosophy

London Rd LLC is built on the belief that real estate investing should be grounded in quality, transparency, and aligned incentives.

Who We Are

London Rd LLC is a California-based real estate investment firm focused on luxury residential development in the San Francisco Bay Area. We create exclusive, off-market opportunities for investors to participate in the equity and value creation behind high-end custom homes.

What We Do

We sponsor and manage select development projects in partnership with Trilink Enterprises Inc., a seasoned luxury home builder. Each project is structured with institutional-grade underwriting, bank financing, and clear investor protections.

Our Builder Partner

Trilink Enterprises Inc. has delivered over a dozen high-end homes across Pleasanton, Walnut Creek, and Woodside. Their work is characterized by timeless design, functional architecture, and premium finishes. Our close relationship allows London Rd to source, underwrite, and execute on exceptional opportunities that larger funds overlook.

Our Approach

  • Acquire land in affluent Bay Area neighborhoods
  • Privately fund design, permits, and pre-construction with equity
  • Secure construction financing from lending partners
  • Build and sell custom homes targeting $5M–$7M exit values
  • Distribute returns to LP investors through a structured waterfall

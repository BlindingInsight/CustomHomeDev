Investment Opportunity

London Investments and Trilink Developers are offering select private investors the opportunity to invest in a luxury residential development project in the San Francisco Bay Area. This common equity investment supports the acquisition, design, permitting, and construction of a single-family custom home with a projected sale value of $6 million.

Development Financials

  • Land purchase: $1.2M (50% debt, 50% equity)
  • Design, permits, fees: $250K (equity funded)
  • Construction loan: $2.5M (bank-financed, ~70% LTC)
  • Total Project Cost: ~$4M
  • Exit sale value: $6M+

Investor Distribution Waterfall

  1. Repay senior debt from sales proceeds
  2. Pay LP investors pro-rata 9% annual preferred return (compounded if unpaid)
  3. Return 100% of invested LP capital
  4. Distribute 10% to Sponsor, 90% to LPs until LPs achieve a 15% IRR
  5. Thereafter, split 75% to Sponsor (London Rd LLC), 25% to LPs

The Team

London Rd LLC serves as the General Partner and sponsor for this investment. Based in California, London Rd brings deep real estate expertise and strategic oversight to each project.

Trilink Enterprises Inc. is a luxury home builder with a proven record of delivering high-end custom homes across the San Francisco Bay Area. Their homes consistently sell in the $3M+ price range and feature high-end finishes, energy-efficient design, and excellent architectural detail.
